Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 1, 2026

About This Project

TweetAgent is a beta student project built for educational purposes. It was created as a learning exercise to explore social media automation, API integrations, and full-stack web development. This is not a commercial product and is not intended for production use at scale.

What We Collect

When you connect your accounts, we store the following data to enable the service:

  • OAuth access tokens for Twitter (X) and LinkedIn, used solely to post on your behalf
  • Your display name and profile identifier from connected platforms
  • Content you generate (tweets, LinkedIn posts) and posting history
  • API keys you provide (Anthropic, Perplexity) to power AI features
  • Basic settings and preferences (topics, schedule, timezone)

How We Use Your Data

  • To generate and post content to your connected social media accounts
  • To schedule automated posts at your chosen times
  • To display your posting history on the dashboard

We do not sell, share, or transfer your data to any third parties. Your API keys and tokens are stored locally on our server and are never exposed to other users.

Third-Party Services

TweetAgent integrates with the following services using your provided credentials:

  • Twitter (X) API — for posting tweets and OAuth authentication
  • LinkedIn API — for posting content and OAuth authentication
  • Anthropic (Claude) — for AI-powered content generation
  • Perplexity — for news research and topic analysis

Each service has its own privacy policy. We encourage you to review them.

Data Storage & Security

  • All data is stored in a local SQLite database on the server
  • Connections are encrypted via HTTPS (TLS)
  • OAuth tokens are stored server-side and never sent to the browser
  • This is a student project — while we take reasonable precautions, we cannot guarantee enterprise-level security

Your Rights

  • You can disconnect your Twitter or LinkedIn account at any time from the dashboard
  • You can request deletion of all your data by contacting us
  • You can stop automated posting at any time by toggling off the agent
  • Disconnecting an account revokes our access to post on your behalf
